Introduction

Coffee is this beautiful, magical bean that, when transformed into a beverage, can make even Grumpy the dwarf much happier.

When consumed, coffee can help people feel more awake and give them a boost of energy. While some people consume coffee for its benedits, others simply like the taste and asthetic of it.

Different Types

Here are a few different methods in which one can consume coffee:

  • Cold Coffee
  • Hot Coffee

Steps

Here are the steps you can use to create coffee drinks.

  1. Gather supplies
  2. Brew espresso
  3. Mix into milk
  4. Add your choice of sweetner
  5. For cold coffee, top with ice

Toppings

After you prepare your cold or hot coffee drink, you can add any toppings to it.

Most people choose to top their drink with whipped cream. You can also add caramel drizzle, chocolate drizzle, praline, nutmeg, and cinnamon.

You can add these toppings either below or on top of the whipped cream.

Activities

Once you place your toppings, you can enjoy your coffee drink while doing any activiy.

You can use your laptop, watch television, scroll through tiktok, pet your dog, or paint your nails.

Whichever activity you choose to do, just remember that coffee is our savior.
